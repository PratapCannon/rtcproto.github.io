Rig Tshoel – Research Journal of the Royal Thimphu College

Rig Tshoel is an open-access peer-reviewed journal that aligns with the key mission of Royal Thimphu College: "To be a crucible of new ideas and knowledge that serves to enrich people's lives and enhances the welfare of society."

The journal aims to issue at least once a year and invites contributions on a wide range of subjects, both from within and outside the RTC community. Please visit the journal website for complete details and current/past issues.
